About Me

I'm Anusha — an AI student, storyteller, and creative soul navigating the intersection of technology and expression. Currently pursuing BSc (Hons) Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence at Sunway College Kathmandu, I'm deeply curious about how machines learn and how humans connect.


Beyond the code, I'm a cinephile who finds poetry in cinema, a writer who believes words carry magic, and a dreamer who builds little corners of the internet that feel like home. My journey is about blending logic with imagination, data with drama, and algorithms with art.


I love hosting events, creating content, voiceover work, and sharing stories that matter. Whether it's through a blog post, a video, or a conversation — I'm here to create, collaborate, and grow.
